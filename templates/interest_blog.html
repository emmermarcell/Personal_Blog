Interests

Great Series and Movies

  • Jojo's Bizarre Adventure - An anime series that follows the adventures of the Joestar family as they battle supernatural villains using their unique powers.
  • Cowboy Bebop - An anime series set in the year 2071, following the adventures of a group of bounty hunters traveling on their spaceship, the Bebop.
  • Japanese Tales of the Macabre - A collection of short horror stories from Japan, featuring ghosts, demons, and other supernatural beings.
  • Cabinet of Curiosities - A Netflix series that explores the strange and unusual artifacts and stories from around the world.
  • Asobi Asobase - An anime series that follows the absurd and humorous everyday lives of three high school girls in a gaming club.

Book Recommendations

  • Dune by Frank Herbert - A science-fiction novel set in a distant future, where noble houses fight for control of a desert planet called Arrakis, which is the only source of a valuable substance called "spice".
  • The Stranger by Albert Camus - A philosophical novel that explores the themes of existentialism and absurdism, following the story of a man named Meursault who kills an Arab and faces the consequences.
  • Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman! by Richard Feynman - An autobiographical book by the physicist Richard Feynman, which describes his life and work in an entertaining and humorous way.
  • Physics and Beyond by Werner Heisenberg - A book by the Nobel Prize-winning physicist Werner Heisenberg, which explores the philosophical implications of quantum mechanics and the role of science in society.
